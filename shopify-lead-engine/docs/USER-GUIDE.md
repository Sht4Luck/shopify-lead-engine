## Outreach Best Practices

### Email Template Structure

```
Subject: Quick feedback on [Store Name]

Hi [Name if you can find it],

I came across [Store Name] while researching [niche] brands, 
and [specific compliment about something they do well].

[AI-GENERATED HOOK - personalized observation]

[YOUR OFFER - based on AI suggestion but in your words]

Would you be open to a quick chat this week?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Portfolio/Website]
```

### DO's

‚úÖ **Personalize Every Email**
- Reference specific products
- Mention something unique about their brand
- Use their brand voice/tone

‚úÖ **Lead With Value**
- Compliment first
- Identify opportunity (not criticism)
- Offer free sample work

‚úÖ **Keep It Short**
- 3-4 paragraphs maximum
- Respect their time
- Easy to skim

‚úÖ **Follow Up Once**
- Wait 5-7 days
- Reference original email
- Don't be pushy

‚úÖ **Track Everything**
- Update lead status
- Note response rates
- Learn what works

### DON'Ts

‚ùå **Don't Use Templates**
- AI hook is a starting point
- Rewrite in your voice
- Add genuine personal touches

‚ùå **Don't Be Critical**
- Frame as opportunity
- Avoid "your copy is bad"
- Focus on potential gains

‚ùå **Don't Spam**
- One email per store
- One follow-up maximum
- Respect opt-outs immediately

‚ùå **Don't Auto-Send**
- Review every lead manually
- Customize each message
- This tool is for research, not automation

‚ùå **Don't Over-Explain**
- They don't need to know about AI
- Keep it about them
- Save details for later

### Platform-Specific Tips

**Email**:
- Best for initial outreach
- Professional tone
- Include portfolio

**Instagram DM**:
- More casual approach
- Reference their content
- Build relationship first

**Contact Form**:
- Keep very brief
- Include clear CTA
- Link to your work

## Managing Your Pipeline

### Status Tracking

Use the status dropdown for each lead:

**Not Contacted**:
- Default status
- Leads you haven't reached out to yet
- Prioritize by score

**Contacted**:
- You've sent initial outreach
- Awaiting response
- Follow up if no reply in 7 days

**Client**:
- They responded positively
- In discussion or working together

## Tips & Tricks

### Maximizing Response Rates

1. **Research Beyond the Tool**
   - Check their social media
   - Read recent blog posts
   - Find the owner's name
   - Reference specific products

2. **Timing Matters**
   - Tuesday-Thursday: Best days
   - 10am-2pm: Peak open times
   - Avoid Mondays and Fridays
   - Never send weekends (unless B2C)

3. **Subject Lines**
   - Keep under 50 characters
   - Be specific: "[Store Name] copy feedback"
   - Avoid spam words: "FREE", "SALE", etc.
   - A/B test different approaches

4. **Show Your Work**
   - Include portfolio link
   - Share case studies
   - Show before/after examples
   - Demonstrate results

### Finding Decision Makers

**Clues in the Store**:
- "About Us" page
- Blog posts (author names)
- Customer service emails
- Social media profiles

**External Research**:
- LinkedIn company page
- WHOIS domain lookup
- Social media bios
- Business registries

**Making Contact**:
- Address to "Store Owner" if no name
- Avoid generic "info@" emails
- DM Instagram if active
- Use contact form as backup

### Qualifying Leads Better

**Good Indicators**:
- Active social media (posted recently)
- Professional website design
- Clear product photography
- Regular blog/content updates
- Good review volume

**Red Flags**:
- Abandoned stores (old dates)
- No social media
- Unprofessional design
- Dropshipping indicators
- No original content

### Building Credibility

**Before Outreach**:
- Create portfolio website
- Develop case studies
- Get testimonials
- Show your process

**In Your Email**:
- Link to relevant work
- Mention similar clients
- Reference your expertise
- Be professional

## Common Questions

### How many leads should I contact per day?

**Recommendation**: 5-10 personalized emails/day

**Why**:
- Quality > quantity
- Personalization takes time
- Better response rates
- Sustainable pace

### What's a good response rate?

**Industry Average**: 5-15%

**Factors That Help**:
- Strong personalization
- Relevant portfolio
- Good timing
- Clear value prop

**Your Goal**: Aim for 10%+

### Should I offer free work?

**Yes, but strategically**:
- ‚úÖ One product description rewrite
- ‚úÖ Homepage copy audit
- ‚úÖ Email sequence review
- ‚ùå Full site rewrite
- ‚ùå Entire email funnel
- ‚ùå Unlimited revisions

**Why It Works**:
- Demonstrates skill
- Low risk for them
- Foot in the door
- Shows results

### How do I price my services?

**After Getting Response**:
- Don't quote in first email
- Discuss scope on call
- Provide custom quote
- Offer packages

**Typical Pricing**:
- Product descriptions: $50-150 each
- Homepage copy: $500-2000
- Email sequences: $1000-3000
- Full site: $3000-10000+

### Can I use this for cold outreach?

**Yes, ethically**:
- ‚úÖ Personalized emails
- ‚úÖ Genuine value proposition
- ‚úÖ Respect opt-outs
- ‚úÖ Follow CAN-SPAM Act
- ‚ùå Never automated
- ‚ùå Never spam lists
- ‚ùå Never purchased data

### What if they don't respond?

**Follow-Up Strategy**:
1. Wait 5-7 days
2. Send brief follow-up
3. Reference original email
4. Add new value point
5. If no response, move on

**Never**:
- Send more than 2 emails
- Contact via multiple channels
- Be pushy or aggressive
- Take it personally

### How do I handle rejections?

**Professional Response**:
```
Thanks for letting me know! 
No problem at all.

If anything changes, feel free 
to reach out.

Best of luck with [Store Name]!
```

**Then**:
- Update status
- Move to next lead
- Don't take personally
- Learn from feedback

### Can I share leads with others?

**Ethical Considerations**:
- This is public data
- But respect privacy
- Don't sell lead lists
- Don't spam on behalf of others

**Better Approach**:
- Help them set up own instance
- Show them this tool
- Don't share raw data
